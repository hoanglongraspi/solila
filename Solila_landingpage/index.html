E-LEARNING COMPANION


Through educational software and supporting devices, SOLILA provides a communication gateway between teachers, parents, and students, enhancing the productivity of students.

FOR SCHOOLS

Teachers will be assisted with the digitalizing process of assigning homework, marking, and managing students' after-class sessions. Therefore, they will gain efficient shortcuts in the daily workflow to improve the overall schooling quality.

FOR PARENTS

Parents are provided with systematic and real-time update children's on-class and self-learning progress. Besides, they will receive their children's monthly study report with grades, rankings and progress-based report to understand their children's potential.

FOR STUDENTS

Students will be encouraged and managed after-class study process. In addition, self-study materials based on assignments plan and on-class study progress will be suggested.
